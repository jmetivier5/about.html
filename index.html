Who Am I?

Hey, I’m Josh! A second-year GBDA student attending the University of Waterloo.


I have always been passionate about design. From a young age, I’ve loved to draw and create. Stemming off of this as I grew older, I learned many different types of design. Some of these include Illustration, Fashion design, Graphic design, Product design, and UX/UI design.


My personality reflects in my design style quite a bit. I am a perfectionist down to the small details and I love to learn new areas of design that can be incorporated into my work. I enjoy creating custom typefaces and lettering as well as putting together collage and poster pieces.


Some of my favorite time spent on design has been on my clothing brand “Star Kreutz” (originally “Fallen Sky Apparel”). It is currently undergoing a complete rebrand which I am excited to release to the public. For this project I have had to do market research, understanding product design, graphic design, and web design. Every day I learn something new that adds to my skillset and helps me improve my brand.


I have developed these skills through the journey of my brand and have been able to put them to use in a business that generates revenue which I reinvest back into the brand as well as into my barbering business.


Being an entrepreneur from such a young age has helped with my understanding and knowledge of time and resource management. I have to plan things out accordingly to ensure that customers aren’t left waiting or that there isn’t enough capital to invest into the next collection.


I’ve gained team collaboration skills while working on a group UX/UI project where we created wireframes, and digital prototypes for a new function/mode in a pre-existing platform. This has taught me leadership and collaborative skills that add to my knowledge.
